Privacy Policy



This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their 'Personally Identifiable Information' (PII) is being used online. PII, as used in US privacy law and information security, is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our application.

 

What personal information do we collect from the people who use Talking Alarm?

 

When using Talking Alarm, none of your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is sent to us. We DO NOT collect any of the data that are stored or generated while using Talking Alarm. To generate the audio file that will be played when the alarm is dismissed, the text is sent directly to the Text-To-Speech server. It (the ttext) resides there the time of its conversion and is deleted right after the audio file is ready. The audio file remains on the server for approximatively 10 seconds before being deleted as well. For more information visit TTS-API.COM and TTSENGINE.COM

   

What permissions are required for Talking Alarm to run efficiently?

 

When you run Talking Alarm for the first time, the application will ask your permission to access the following:
 

  • Calendar
  • Location
  • Shared Files
  • Connect to BBM
  • Run Headless
 

Why does Talking Alarm need to access them?

 
  • Location prenission is required to get the weather forecast of your location. If this permission is denied, Talking Alarm will not be able to set the shop location.

  • Calendar access is required to display youe upcoming events and populate the events widget. If this permission is denied, Talking Alarm will not be able to get and display your upcoming events.

  • Shared Files is required if you want to set your own ringtones, audio files located on your device or you SD Card. If this permission is denied, you will not be able to set personalized ringtones in Talking Alarm.

  • Run Headless is required for Talking Alarm to run headless. If this permission is denied Talking Alarm will not work at all.

  • Connect to BBM is required for updating your BBM status. If denied, Talking Alarm will not be able to update your BBM status.
