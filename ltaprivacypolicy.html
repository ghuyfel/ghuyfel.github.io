This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their 'Personally Identifiable Information' (PII) is being used online. PII, as used in US privacy law and information security, is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our application.
 
What personal information do we collect from the people who use List Them All?
 
When using List Them All, none of your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is sent to us. We DO NOT collect any of the data that are stored or generated while using List Them All. Everything remains on your BlackBerry device.
 
 
What permissions are required for List Them All to run efficiently?
 
When you run List Them All for the first time, the application will ask your permission to access the following:
 
  • Location
  • Notebooks
  • Shared Files
  • Connect to BBM
  • Camera
 
Why does List Them All need to access them?
 
  • Location prenission is required in the Window Shopping List activity to access the shop location when saving a new item. If this permission is denied, List Them All will not be able to set the shop location.
  • Notebooks access is required to add entries in the Remember application when setting a reminder on your shopping list. If this permission is denied, List Them All will not be able to add new entries in the Remember application.
  • Shared Files is required to save exported shopping lists onto your device or you SD Card, and access pictures taken by your camera  during Window Shopping activities. If this permission is denied, List Them All will be enable to save export your list and to access your pictures.
  • Camera is required for the Window Shopping activity. If denied, List Them All will not be able to access and save items' images.
  • Connect to BBM is required for updating your BBM status. If denied, List Them All will not be able to update your BBM status.
